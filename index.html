Welcome to our Family Tattoo Studio

Family Tattoo Studio founded in 1996 in Kyiv. From first steps we decide to show tattoo as art. We believe that everybody has the right to express themselves through tattoos and we help them with beauty and art. Our studio has more than 10 tattoo artists, each working in different styles and individual techniques. You can choose any you like: old school, new school, black &grey , colored realism, portrait, watercolor, dotwork, linework, graphic and come for the session to the artist you liked most. We give you high quality of work but what’s more important is that we give you safety and sterility. Our artists visit special trainings where they learn about all new European safety and client’s health standards.

Also in our studio you can get:

  • permanent make-up
  • piercing of any kind
  • laser tattoo removal
  • henna and jagua-gel tattoos
  • Tattoo School education in different areas: tattoo artist, permanent make-up artist, piercing artist.
